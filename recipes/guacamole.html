Simple Guacamole

Description

A simple guacamole recipe to make your life easier.

Ingredients:

  • 5 avocados - peeled, pitted and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup minced green onion
  • 1/2 cup minced fresh cilantro
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. Stir together the avocado and lemon juice in a serving bowl.
  2. Add the green onion and cilantro. Mix well.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve immediately or store covered in refrigerator.
  5. Keep the avocado pits in the bowl to keep from browning.
