Cauliflower Pizza Crust

Description

A cauliflower pizza is a healthier alternative to regular flour pizza. Give it a try!

Ingredients:

  • 1 head cauliflower, cored and separated into florets
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ounces fresh goat cheese
  • 1/2 ounce finely grated parmigiano-reggiano cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Puree cauliflower in a food processor until finely ground.
  4. Cook ground cauliflower, water and salt together in a skillet over medium to high heat until fragrant and most of the water evaporates. Let cool.
  5. Transfer cauliflower to the center of a clean dish towel.
  6. Wrap and squeeze tightly to remove all excess moisture. You should have about 1 1/2 cups cauliflower pulp.
  7. Stir cauliflower, goat cheese, parmigiano-reggiano cheese, egg and cayenne pepper together in a bowl to form a soft dough.
  8. Gather dough in a ball and place in the center of the prepared baking sheet. Press dough into circle about 1/4 inch thick.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and set about 40 minutes.
  10. Cool and turn crust over before topping!
